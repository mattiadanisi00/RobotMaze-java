Info

It is easy. The robot has to get from the first cell in the top left, to the last cell in the top right, between narrow spaces and mood changes!

Red means mad
He has been locked up in the maze and is running madly toward the exit, one cell at a time, calculating the shortest route.

Green means calm
He remembered that he did not need food and water. He takes his time and walks to the exit, calculating the shortest route, one cell at a time

Yellow means vigorous
He takes advantage of recent upgrades made to his robo-legs by jumping two cells at a time to the exit, taking the shortest route

Cyan means panic
It is a claustrophobic robot. It doesn't like enclosed, tight spaces. It runs randomly looking for the exit

Grey means step
Little robot-ghosts. They remind the robot of the steps it took to get out of the maze
